About Abishek Mahi

Abishek Mahendhiran, better known by his stage name Abishek Mahi was born on 7 August 2001 in Krishnagiri. He is an Indian Entrepreneur, Independent Artist, Song writer and YouTuber. He is the founder of Isai Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. and Isai Tamil Records. He started his career on YouTube by composing and uploading music videos. In 2020, he started the independent music label to distribute the independent artists songs and albums.
